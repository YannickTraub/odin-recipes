(WIP) Paella à la Papa

Home

Description

This recipe makes the absolute best paella in the world. Since I was a child, my dad occasionally cooked this for the whole family and till this day everybody is overjoyed whenever he makes it again.

With this recipe, I'm trying to preserve it forever, although I don't know if anybody can make it as good as the original. For the best taste, it's important to make it in one of those big paella pans.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Pork
  • Lamb
  • Prawns
  • Green Beans
  • Paprika
  • Saffron

Steps

  1. Cut everything
  2. Let Rice simmer
  3. Add all other ingredients
  4. Season everything
  5. Let it simmer more
  6. Eat
