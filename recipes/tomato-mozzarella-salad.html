Tomato Mozzarella Salad

Home

Description

A quick and easy tomato mozzarella salad. Perfect for a light and healthy side to a savory main course or as a quick summer snack.

With not much in it, it's paramount to use quality ingredients for the best taste. Especially a good balsamic vinegar can make or break the recipe. I usually use herbal salt to season it, so experiment with different brands to find your favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 mozzarella
  • 4 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • basil
  • herbal salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the tomatoes into slices. I recommend thinner ones as they make for a better mouth feel
  2. Cut the mozzarella into similar sized slices.
  3. Either layer them slightly next to each other if you want a better visual experience or just mix them in a bowl for better seasoning coverage
  4. Mix the vinegar and olive oil and pour it over the tomatoes and mozzarella.
  5. Season with herbal salt and pepper to your taste
  6. Add basil leaves on top
