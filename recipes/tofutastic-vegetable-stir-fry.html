Tofutastic Vegetable Stir-Fry

Home

Description

This is a incredibly tasty and easy to make vegetable stir-fry with tofu on a rich-bodied soy sauce base.
It is a staple recipe in my collection and a fan-favorite among friends and family.

The vegetables included here are my personal favorites and fit the flavor profile well, but it's perfectly fine to add others.
The savory tofu gives it some good crunch and adds vegan-friendly proteins while the soy sauce gives it the umami flavor

Ingredients

  • 2 Zucchinis
  • 4 Carrots
  • 1 Spring Onion
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 Gloves garlic
  • 400g Smoked Tofu
  • 120g Jasmin Rice
  • Turmeric, cumin, pepper, ground coriander, nutmeg, cinnamon, chili flakes
  • Soy sauce
  • Cooking oil
  • Peanut oil
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Wash the vegetables, peel if necessary, and slice into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Finely chop the onion and garlic.
  3. Wrap tofu in paper towels and press out the water. Then cut into bite-sized cubes.
  4. Heat some oil in a large (wok) pan and add the carrots.
    • After a while, add onion and garlic and sauté briefly.
    • Finally, add zucchini and spring onion, and cook everything over medium heat until desired tenderness.
    • Season the vegetables with cumin, coriander, pepper, and a generous amount of turmeric.
    • Tip: If the vegetables brown too quickly but aren't yet tender, deglaze with a bit of water or vegetable broth and let simmer until done.
  5. Meanwhile, heat oil in another pan and fry the tofu cubes over medium to high heat for 6-8 minutes.
    • Don't stir too often so a crust can form.
    • After about halfway through, season with salt, pepper, turmeric, and cumin, and finish cooking over medium heat.
  6. Cook the rice in parallel.
  7. Add the cooked tofu to the cooked vegetables, mix well, and optionally season again with turmeric, pepper, coriander, and a bit of peanut oil.
  8. Deglaze over low heat with soy sauce, then season with nutmeg, cinnamon, and chili flakes.
  9. Serve the “Tofutastic Vegetable Stir-Fry” over Asian grains with a red or rose wine of your choice.
